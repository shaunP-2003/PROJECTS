About Us


Welcome to our fish and chips shop, where the flavors of the western cape come alive!

With deep roots in our coastal community, we carry on the tradition of perfectly crispy batter and succulent fish that has been cherished for generations.
Sourcing the freshest catches from local fisherman, we honor sustainability while delivering an authentic taste of our heritage.

Join us in savoring the timeless delight of fish and chips and experience the warm hospitality of our town.
Welcome to a culinary journey rooted in tradition and flavor!


Hawston, Western Cape

Our beautiful hometown. The origin of our delicious recipes.
